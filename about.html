ABOUT US

A Leading Stone Exporter In India.

Aastha Natural Stones is a certified Indian natural stone exporter that exports high-quality Indian natural stones including Granite, Marble, Quartz slabs, and stone artefacts. We deliver stones from North as well as South India. We source Raw stones directly from quarries for blocks and Processed natural stones from manufacturers for slabs for all your customized requirements (available in finishes like natural, honed, polished, calibrated, brushed, sandblasted, and tumbled in hand-cut or machine-cut edges from multiwire/gangsaw/cutter setups).

We are known for our best sourcing, bargain, and quality control on behalf of our clients from various manufacturers as the production of natural stone varieties is practically impossible from a single factory hence we prefer to buy stones from factories situated near the quarries in different regions. Our trained field staff and quality control manager manage not only the best quality production along with the unmatched prices but also the packaging and transporting to the ports.

North Indian stones are exported from Mundra and Mumbai ports while South Indian stones are exported from Chennai or Tuticorin ports. We have supplied an array of stones to numerous construction projects all over the world. Our competency lies in our ability to clearly understand the customer's requirements and fulfill them in a way that would certainly bind him/her to deal and recommend us in the future. We offer a host of services that range from helping our customers to select the right stone, quality control, fast and timely deliveries, complete transparency in entire transaction communication, and shipment assistance.

ABOUT US

A renowned international natural stone export firm.

North India’s one of the most renowned stone exporters based in Rajasthan (Jaipur), Aastha Natural Stones supplies precision stone products from the heart of India’s mining & stone crafting region. The local traditions of stone palace and temple construction, combined with some of the most unique Sandstone, Marble, Granite & slates quarries gives Aastha Natural Stones a rich heritage to build upon.

The company has been successfully supplying high quality of Granite, Sand-stones and other natural stones and their Artefacts to the major International markets in South Africa, Malaysia and finished products i.e. tiles and slabs to Kuwait, USA , Germany, UAE, Singapore, , Australia to name just a few. Established in the year 2023 , the company is successfully extending its service to the dimensional stone supplies all around world. We Deal in Granite blocks, Gang saw slabs, Cutter size slabs . Wire-saw slabs and various Artifacts and trade more than 10 containers a month.

Aastha Natural Stones is proudly serving its clients in various countries with unparalleled customer retention and reorders. Our Strength is our procurement and supply chain in all major sectors.

Where does Aastha Naturals source its natural stones from?

We source our natural stones from various regions across India, including North and South India. We procure raw stones directly from quarries and processed stones from manufacturers to ensure quality and variety.

What finishes are available for the natural stones provided by Aastha Naturals?

Our natural stones are available in finishes such as natural, honed, polished, calibrated, brushed, sandblasted, and tumbled. We offer both hand-cut and machine-cut edges to cater to your specific requirements.

How does Aastha Naturals ensure quality control for its natural stones?

We have a dedicated team of experts who meticulously assess each stone to guarantee superior quality. Our quality control process starts from sourcing and continues through production, packaging, and transportation.
