Certified Indian Slate Supplier and Exporter
Slate is a homogeneous, fine-grained, foliated metamorphic rock, which originated from sedimentary rock and is formed out of volcanic lava settled over clay deposits. Commonly occurring in different shades of grey, slate also exhibits other colors like purple, cyan, and green. Astha Natural Stones Universal Exports is one of the certified and experienced Indian slate suppliers of tiles and mosaics. Slate roof and tiles offer lots of advantages. Hence, we ensure you get maximum benefits at a price tag that is competitive.
