Basalt Exporter and Supplier in India
Extracted from the natural stone quarries located in the Rajasthan state of Northern India, Basalt is considered a volcanic stone resulting from the volcanic activities beneath the Earth’s crust. Basalt stone is a highly dense natural stone which makes it an ideal choice for various applications. The most widely preferred basalt applications are walkways, flooring, paving, and others.
The majority of the basalt on Earth is formed along divergent plate boundaries in the mid-ocean ridge system. Convection currents transport heated rock from deep into the mantle. As the diverging boundary breaks apart, the heated rock melts and bursts onto the sea bottom. Pillow basalt is frequently produced by undersea fissure eruptions.
